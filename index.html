About Elephants

African Elephant

The African elephant is the largest living terrestrial animal. It is known for its large ears and trunk, which are used for a variety of purposes including feeding, drinking, and social interaction.

Asian Elephant

The Asian elephant is smaller than its African counterpart and has smaller ears. They are found in various countries across Asia and are known for their distinct social structure and behaviors.
